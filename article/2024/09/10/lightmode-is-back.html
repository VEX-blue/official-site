Lightmode is back

By Samantha Xavia


I know yesterday we announced Version 2.3 of the website but today we done even more work on making the website more accessible for everyone. Including bring back Lightmode, but in a slightly different way... We've got a small little toggle on the top right of the desktop website and navigation menu on mobile.



All you need to do to toggle to lightmode and back is click the lightbulb. Just as simple as that, it will also carry accross every page on the site and even across browsing sessions on our site. I feel this is a great edition as there's times that you may not want to use our website in Darkmode such as being out in the sun, reading large bits of text such as our Articles or you are just a straight maniac and prefer lightmode at all times of the day, Just insane.

We've added hover effects (only works on desktop) for;
- Article Cards
- Navbar
- Various Buttons

> We moved the rules area for the Southampton.Social page to a new rules subpage.
> Added a 'Join Us' button for users on the Southampton.Social page
> Added Two new services; Mastodon Hosting & Basic Website Services
> Fixed Spacing for Services on Services Page
> Fixed Portfolios (Video Editing & Podcasts) Spacing in Mobile

Overall there's a lot of updates in less than 24 hours, this is probably as I mainly focused on making this site more optimised and effective in the future. Who doesn't want easier navigation and accessiblity features when it comes to a hopefully great designed website. As all of this progress has been changed along with a huge overhall on how the website reacts to you the user using it i'm glad to say this is Version 2.4 of the site but please don't worry I won't be releasing Version 2.69 for a long time, I plan to keep the website with this style for a long time coming, I just needed to make sure that it was functional and any medium features like toggling lightmode needs to be done before we get to 100+ pages in the future.
